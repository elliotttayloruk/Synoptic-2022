Everything you need to know about Our donations Locker

Locker is a secure, refrigerated, self-service kiosk that allows you to pick up and drop off your donation.

How to use our donations Locker

It’s easy – Users simply go to the Locker drop of location Once a donation is ready for pickup, users will receive an e-mail with a unique 6 digit code that they’ll use to collect/drop off the donation from the designated slot.
