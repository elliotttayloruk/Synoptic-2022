Rewards


We would like to introduce you to our rewards system.

This has been interlinked with local businesses and council

Farmer Discount

Farmers who decide to take part in this project will have their delivery fees of resources covered by/discounted by the local council. Also all compost that is collected will be divided among the farmers to feed animals and improve soil quality.

Certifcates

Young people who volunteer to help with the project for 30 days or receive 10000 Reward points will be eligible to receive a certificate from the local council. To help young people get into further education this will be considered an advantageous certificate that universities in Australia will consider an edge in comparison to other candidates.
